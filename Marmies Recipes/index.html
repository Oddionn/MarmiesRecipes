About the website

Within this website are most of the recipes that I have made for my family. Some of these I got from my Grand-Mom and some from my Mom. I also got some of these from my Mother-in law and from friends, as well as some of my favorite cook books! I hope that through these recipes you will be able to find new favorite dishes for your family! I've always enjoyed cooking and baking, and it was with love that all of these dishes were made. One thing is for sure, I always measured everything! I never wanted to take chances and make mistakes! I put my heart into each of these dishes, and I hope that my family will remember me fondly when making them.
